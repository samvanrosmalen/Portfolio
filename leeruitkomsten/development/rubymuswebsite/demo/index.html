About

My name is Ruby Muss. I am from Kerkrade in The Netherlands and I am 20 years old. I've been taking singing lessons since I was 9 years old, I've been taking piano lessons for 2 years and I've been dancing for 10 years.

My goal is to work towards an EP. With that, I hope to eventually attract the attention of labels, for example via the radio. With my music, I want to let people know that it's normal to be in conflict with who you are, to appreciate yourself and to come out for it. I want to explain that through lyrics about obstacles I encounter and how people can deal with them.
